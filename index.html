Looking for alternative options for post-surgery pain relief?

Surgery is painful no matter where it’s performed on your body. Recovery can be a nightmare especially if the pain is unbearable. While many Western doctors are quick to prescribe addictive pain medications, acupuncture after surgery can help you with post-op pain relief.

Reducing your pain from surgery can start before your procedure, too.

Clinical studies have shown that treatments before and after your surgery significantly reduced pain and the use of painkillers.

Cut your healing time in half.

Healing from surgery can be a long process. Utilizing acupuncture in your recovery can help cut your recovery time in half with most people accelerating healing times by 50-70%.

Your tailored recovery plan.

Each surgery is different, which means each recovery is different. When you choose Pinpoint Acupuncture Clinic to help you with your surgery recovery, we get as much information as possible to help us create a plan to suit your needs. We will have you provide a full health history, talk to you about your surgery, and then come up with a post-surgery plan. We can focus on just acupuncture for pain relief, as well as work in conjunction with any prescribed medications. Studies have shown the use of acupuncture with pain medications can help decrease reliance on pain meds, particularly in helping to lower dosage strength and the overall time on the medications. So acupuncture alone, or in tandem with a prescribed medication, will help no matter what.
