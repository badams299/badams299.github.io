Alleviate, Rejuvenate, Heal

Acupuncture is a centuries-old proven form of medical treatment that has advanced into a complete integrative health care system that helps bridge the gap between traditional methods and contemporary healing systems of medicine. Practitioners of acupuncture and Chinese medicine have used this non-invasive healing method to help support health and wellness around the world. Acupuncture promotes holistic healing by utilizing the body’s endogenous pharmacy, which can fortify immunity, support physical and emotional health, and bring our bodies back to a state of balance and homeostasis. There are no side-effects, it is essentially painless, and can be an effective alternative to prescription drugs and narcotics.

Integrative Acupuncture for Wellness

For many of us when we get sick or become injured, we go to see our doctor and are usually prescribed medication or provided another type of treatment. Although this conventional modality of medicine helps many, it does not always prove to be effective for everyone. Patients can be left feeling hopeless when all of their options have seemingly been used up, and they don’t know what the next step is in their healing process. More recently acupuncture has been recognized as an integrative therapy that can be included within many patient’s treatment plan and fill the void in these situations. It has been observed through research that acupuncture has been proven to help treat:

  • Pain and inflammation
  • Gastrointestinal disorders
  • Mental/ Emotional disorders
  • Aids metabolism
  • Insomnia
  • Boosts Immunity
  • Fertility

Every patient’s disease pattern is unique, which means that every treatment will be different. Customized acupuncture protocols will be implemented after every patient has provided an in-depth health history intake, and all details have been accounted for.

Peri-Operative Acupuncture

The recovery process after an operation can be long and sometimes difficult. Utilizing acupuncture before and after a surgical procedure can help reduce troubling symptoms as well as expedite healing time. Acupuncture has been reported to help fortify and alleviate:

  • Wound healing
  • Decrease pain and inhibit inflammation
  • Accelerate recovery time
  • Boost immune system
  • Scar tissue overgrowth
  • Ease peri-operative stress and anxiety
  • Side effects from anesthesia
    • Nausea
    • Shifts in mood
    • Headaches
    • Toxicity
    • Memory Loss
    • Insomnia

According to the American College of Occupational and Environmental Medicine, the US has 4.5% of the worlds population, and we consume 99% of all the world’s hydrocodone and 80% of oxycodone. Acupuncture is a great holistic alternative to the contemporary painkillers that are prescribed after surgery. We will customize a treatment plan for you, all the while working closely with your surgeon. With minimal to no side effects, integrative acupuncture is a great addition to help enhance your recovery plan.
