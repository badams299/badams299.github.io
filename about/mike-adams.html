Mike Adams, L.Ac.

Licensed Acupuncturist

My mission is to provide the highest level of integrative care in order to produce the best results and outcomes for my patients.

I graduated with a bachelor’s degree from the University of Vermont in Recreation Management and Natural Resources. I also received a minor degree in emergency medicine, as well as wilderness medicine combined with search and rescue. I became interested in Chinese medicine when meeting a family-friend who was an acupuncturist, and learning about the healing benefits that this medicine can provide. I relocated across the country and graduated from Yo San University of Traditional Chinese Medicine in Los Angeles, California. There, I received a Master’s degree in Traditional Chinese Medicine, as well as getting certified in advanced acupuncture and moxibustion at the China Beijing International Acupuncture Training Center.

Acupuncture can help alleviate and heal within many areas of health, however I have decided to focus the advantages that acupuncture carries towards peri-operative care. The surgical-experience is a very stressful time for the body as well as the mind, and acupuncture has proven effects that can help alleviate the challenges that come with the post-operative recovery. Having personally experienced surgery, I understand the necessary steps that are needed to help enhance the recovery process.
